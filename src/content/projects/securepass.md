Hey everyone! 👋

I’m excited to introduce **Secure Pass**, an application designed to empower users with the tools they need to manage their passwords securely. In today’s digital age, having strong and reliable passwords is more important than ever, and Secure Pass aims to make this process straightforward and effective.

### About the Project

Secure Pass is built with **Java** and **Spring Boot**, utilizing **PostgreSQL** for robust data management and **Docker** for easy deployment. This project encapsulates a suite of services focused on password security, ensuring that users can generate, validate, and store passwords without hassle.

### Key Features

- **Password Generator**: Create strong, random passwords that are difficult to crack, ensuring your accounts remain secure.
- **Password Checker**: Validate the strength and security of your existing passwords, providing feedback on how to improve them.
- **Password Storage**: Safely store and manage your passwords in an encrypted format, giving you peace of mind.

### Why This Project?

I recognized the need for a comprehensive password management solution that not only generates secure passwords but also helps users maintain them effectively. Through Secure Pass, I aimed to combine ease of use with essential security features, making it a go-to tool for anyone concerned about their online safety.
